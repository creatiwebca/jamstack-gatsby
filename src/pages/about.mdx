### Who we are

We are a web studio who helps small business and start ups quickly and efficiently transition from in-store to online selling.
Our service include design and development project.

### Our expertise

We believe a brand can make decisions faster by having access to the right knowledge. 
We know how to grow an online presence, acquire customers and increase conversions by 
developing a solid foundation for a growing brand.
  * Shopify account setup, including taxes, shipping rates, notifications.
  * Pages, collections, products, and navigation creation.
  * Mobile-first design & UX in mind.
  * Theme setup as per the design referance. 
  * App Setup & Configuration

### Shopify partner
Serving Shopify ecosystem since 2019, CREATIWEB digital solutions is trusted for delivering high-end product & services, 
from store set-up, app development, migration to marketing digital and SEO.

We work together with you to identify your goals, strengths, and resources and to create a strategy that you can actually implement.
